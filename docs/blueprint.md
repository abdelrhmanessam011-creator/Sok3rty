# **App Name**: Sweet Surprises

## Core Features:

- Countdown Timer: A visually appealing countdown timer displaying the time remaining until your love's birthday.
- Photo Gallery: A beautiful gallery showcasing cherished photos and memories of you and your loved one.
- Love Letter Generator: AI-powered tool that generates heartfelt and personalized love letters, customizable with various prompts.
- Birthday Wish Submission Form: A form allowing friends and family to submit birthday wishes, displayed on a dedicated page.
- Guest Book: A guest book that allows for persistent wishes. No database implementation specified.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft lavender (#E6E6FA) to evoke feelings of tenderness and affection.
- Background color: Light cream (#F5F5DC) to provide a warm and inviting backdrop.
- Accent color: Dusty rose (#D8BFD8) for highlights and call-to-action buttons, complementing the lavender hue.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif font for elegance.
- Use delicate and romantic icons, such as hearts, flowers, and stars, to enhance the theme.
- Implement subtle and heartwarming animations, such as fading effects and gentle transitions, to add a touch of magic.
